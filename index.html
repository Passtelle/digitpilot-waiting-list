Your journey

From curious to confident trader

DigitPilot combines structured lessons, a risk free market simulator and an AI co pilot so you can learn at your own pace without putting your savings in danger.

Step 1

Guided learning

Start with the basics. What is a wallet, how do exchanges work and how do you place your first order. Follow a clear path with videos, short texts and simple checklists created for beginners.

Step 2

Practice without risk

Enter the DigitPilot Sandbox, a market simulator that replays historical data. Test your ideas, build execution logic and make mistakes with play money instead of real cash.

Step 3

AI co pilot on call

Ask questions at any time. Your AI co pilot explains sudden market moves, helps you read the chart and walks you through what happened in your simulated trades so you understand what worked and what did not.
